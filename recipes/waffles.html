Waffles Recipe

Description

This waffle recipe is the only one you'll need to make homemade waffles with your waffle iron. Simple pantry ingredients mix up quickly in this easy batter that can be used right away or stored in the refrigerator for up to a week. Serve waffles hot with whipped cream and fresh fruit or with butter and maple syrup for either breakfast, brunch, or a snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoon baking powder
  • nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufaturer's instruction.
  2. Whisk eggs in a large bowll until light and fluffy.
  3. Add flour, milk and vegetable oil and mix to combine.
  4. Spray the preheated waffle iron with nonstick spray.
  5. Pour batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden brown and the iron stops steaming.
